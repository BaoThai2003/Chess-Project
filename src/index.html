Chess Project

Bao, an IT student from Vietnam, traveled to Japan to visit his girlfriend Shizuku, a professional chess player.

During a summer festival at an ancient temple, they discovered a mysterious glowing chessboard...

As Shizuku touched it, a powerful force pulled her inside. Bao jumped after her without hesitation.

They awakened in Akh'Zahara - the Land of Sand.

A world where the working class suffers under intellectual tyrants, and all conflicts are decided by deadly chess battles.

"To find Shizuku and escape this world, you must fight through the arena..."
